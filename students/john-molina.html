Biography

I grew up in a few different towns within Hudson County playing my guitar in a bunch of bands and making short comedy videos with my friends. While in college, I realized how difficult it could be to make ends meet while pursuing a career in music and film. I decided to drop out and find something that could fill my desire to create while earning a decent living. I soon after found out about a Computer Science program at my local Community College. After many sleepless nights and a few years later, I graduated with my Associate's degree in CS. I'm now furthering my knowledge in web development here at the Flatiron School.
